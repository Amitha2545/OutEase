About Outpass Management System

This system aims to simplify and digitize the traditional outpass application process in academic institutions.

With role-based logins (Student, Caretaker, Warden, Security), it provides transparency, speed, and ease for everyone involved.

Female students are also guided to upload undertakings, ensuring proper documentation and security.
